Rules of the Game!

The Game is simple maze game with nine levels.
  • The player needs to enter a username
  • Upon entering the username, the player is allowed to
    • Resume the Game
    • Restart the game or
    • Check the Rules
  • The 🤖is the start of the maze. The Door(🚪) is the destination.
  • The player needs to take the key[🔑] in the maze to open the door else the player cannot complete the game.
  • This is a time based game, the player has only few number of seconds to complete the level. Upon completion of the level, the player can
    • Move to the next level
    • Restart the game(by level 1)
  • The player can try to increase the number of seconds to complete the game by
    • Finding these Tresures[🌹 , 🌟, 💝, 🌸, 🎉]( Time Increases by 10 seconds)
    • Finding the key( Time Increases by 10 seconds)
  • The player's time gets decreased when they find a monster[🐍, 🐊] (Time decreases by 10 seconds). So please

    beware!!

  • If the player is not able to complete the level, the player can
    • Play the level again
    • Restart the game(from level 1)
  • The player score is calculated on these factors
    • The number of treasures collected
    • Key found
    • Number of seconds left before the level is completed
  • The player score is carry forwarded in between levels to calculate the high score of the game.
  • The player is also allowed to login by another username! where the game starts from level one
